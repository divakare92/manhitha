About Manhitha

We help you find the right AI tool — without the hype, without the fluff.

Our Mission

The AI tools market is exploding — there are thousands of options, and most reviews are either sponsored fluff or written by people who haven't actually used the product. Manhitha exists to cut through the noise.

We personally test every tool we review. We spend real time with each product, evaluate it against clear criteria, and tell you exactly what we find — both the good and the bad.

How We Review

Every review on this site follows the same process:

  • Hands-on testing — We use the tool for at least a week before writing
  • Clear criteria — Quality, pricing, ease of use, support, and value for money
  • Side-by-side comparisons — We test tools against their direct competitors
  • Regular updates — AI tools change fast; we keep our reviews current

Affiliate Disclosure

Transparency matters. Here's exactly how we make money:

  • Some links on this site are affiliate links. If you click one and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you.
  • We only recommend tools we've personally tested and genuinely believe in.
  • Affiliate relationships never influence our ratings or reviews. If a tool is bad, we say so — regardless of commission.
  • We also earn from display ads and sponsored content, which is always clearly labeled.

Why Trust Us

We don't accept payment for positive reviews. Period. Every tool on this site is rated based on our actual experience using it. If we wouldn't spend our own money on it, we won't recommend it to you.
