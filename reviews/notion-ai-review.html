Notion AI Review: Is the AI Add-on Worth $10/Month?

★★★★☆ 4.5/5 — Good, but not essential

We used Notion AI for a month across real team workflows. Here's when it shines and when you should skip.

This review contains affiliate links.

Quick Verdict

Notion AI is impressive but not essential. If you already live in Notion and write a lot of documentation, it's worth the $10/month. If you use Notion casually, skip it — the free plan is plenty.

What's Good

  • AI writing assistant — Draft, edit, summarize, and translate inside your docs
  • Smart Q&A — Ask questions about your workspace and get answers from your docs
  • Auto-fill databases — Generate properties and summaries for database entries
  • Seamless integration — It feels native, not bolted on

What's Not

  • $10/month extra — On top of any Notion plan (or even the free plan)
  • Not revolutionary — ChatGPT can do most of this for free
  • Limited context — Only sees current page, not your full workspace
  • Occasionally slow — AI responses can take 5-10 seconds

Who Should Buy It

Buy it if you're a writer, project manager, or team that creates lots of documentation inside Notion. Skip it if you primarily use Notion as a simple to-do list or database.
